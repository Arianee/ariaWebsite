The Arianee Protocol Currency

Aria is the native cryptocurrency of the Arianee Protocol. It is a core component of the Protocol's architecture and is essential to its proper operation. Aria enables the economic incentives which reinforce the decentralization of the Arianee Protocol. To learn more about the aria token, read our tokenomics and go to the FAQ.

It is available on Ethereum under the ticker ARIA20.
