Token Distribution

200 million aria were generated at launch.
70 million aria were allocated to token sales (private and public) to fund the early development of the projet.
40 million aria were allocated to the team and early advisors who helped teh project.
10 million aria were allocated to Community incenties.
10 million aria were allocated to an Operational Reserve to run tests and to fund the liquidity pools.
80 million aria are kept in a long-term reserve.
